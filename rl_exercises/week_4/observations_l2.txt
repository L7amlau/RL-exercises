Week 4 - Level 2 Observations

For Level 2, I evaluated the same base configuration across 5 seeds and aggregated the metrics with rliable.

The key takeaway is that the spread across seeds is large (best 446.1, worst 192.4), so a single-seed conclusion would have been too optimistic. IQM is 232.7 (95% CI [196.67, 385.70]), which is more robust than looking at the mean alone.

That mean and median are equal here is expected in this single-task setup with rliable. Overall, the results are more reliable than in Level 1 because uncertainty and variance are explicitly quantified.

Short conclusion: Multi-seed evaluation plus CI and IQM gives a more realistic picture and increases confidence in the interpretation.
