Week 4 - Level 1 Observations

I tested DQN on CartPole-v1 with one seed (0) and 20000 frames. I compared five configurations with different network sizes, replay buffer sizes, and batch sizes.

The best final result came from mlp_64x64_buf10k_bs32 with avg_reward_10 = 264.9. A larger batch size (64) was slightly faster early on, but dropped clearly at the end. A smaller replay buffer (5k instead of 10k) also reduced final performance.

Larger or deeper networks were not automatically better. The 64x64x64 network reached high peaks, but was less stable.

Conclusion: In this Level-1 setup, 64x64 with buffer 10k and batch size 32 is the most robust choice. However, these results are only a first indication because they are based on a single seed.
